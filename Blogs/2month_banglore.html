2 months in Bengaluru

So its been 2 months since I moved to Bengaluru…



What a ride it’s been! Moving from Lucknow to this fast-paced city for my studies has been nothing short of an emotional roller-coaster. New traditions, a different language, and so many new faces — it’s been a major shift from the comfort of home. Leaving my parents and friends behind wasn’t easy, and there were plenty of moments when I found myself thinking about the good old days with a sense of nostalgia.

It took me a solid month to adjust ( survive ), bcz

1. Almost got scammed twice (rookie mistake)
2. Cabs are ridiculously expensive
3. Hard water struggles (RO water hair wash, anyone?)
4. Food’s pricey and, well, not great
5. The public transport system is a maze
6. Traffic? Oh, it’s a feature, not a bug.

But now that I’m comfortable, I’ve made some incredible friends, already created a ton of memories, and yes, survived my mid-terms! Oh, and did I mention I’ve had the privilege to meet some of the most influential people in the country? Including:

- Amod Malviya (co-founder of udaan.com)
- Akash Saxena (CTO of JioCinema)
- Varun Mohan (CEO of Codeium)
- Ankit Goyal ( Vice President Paytm )
- Pulkit Jain ( Co-Founder of Vedantu ) and many more….

This is just the beginning. I’m embracing the change, settling into Bangalore life, and pushing myself to make the most out of my journey with Scaler School of Technology.
